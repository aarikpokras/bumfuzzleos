If you just downloaded spectacleOS, please run the following commands:

1. Exit the spectacleos directory, into the containing directory.

`chmod -R 755 spectacleos spectacleos/bin`

`./first-time`, and follow the directions.

`./specs start` to start spectacleOS.

See the wiki for important information.
https://github.com/aarikpokras/spectacleos/wiki
